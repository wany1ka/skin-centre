CRYOTHERAPY TREATMENT

Also known as Cryosurgery or skin tag removal, ... Cryotherapy is effective in the removal of abnormal skin cells through applying extremely cold liquid or instrument by dermatologists and primary care doctors to significantly lower the air temperature around the patient to promote of the wellness and beauty to new heights. We apply Cryotherapy to freeze and destroy targeted skin growths while preserving the surrounding skin, eliminate aches and pains from suffered injuries (both recent and old), skin disorders, cellulite, saggy skin as well as those seeking to prevent full body ageing. This medical procedure is usually employed to destroy a variety of benign skin growths, such as warts and age spots, pre-cancerous lesions like actinic keratoses, and malignant lesions such as basal cell and squamous cell cancers. Cryotherapy is greatly beneficial since it reduces migraine symptoms, numbs nerve irritation, reduces arthritic pain, may help treat low-risk tumors, helps prevent dementia and Alzheimer's disease, treats atopic dermatitis and other skin conditions and helps treat mood disorders. Cryotherapy poses little risk to elderly and other patients who may not be good candidates for other surgical procedures since it does not pose the risk of scarring, infection, and damage to underlying skin and tissue.
